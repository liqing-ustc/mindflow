☕ Make Coffee & Check Schedule
User: "Make me a coffee and check my afternoon schedule."
1
Brain Parse instruction → push 2 goals: [make_coffee, check_schedule]
2
CLI Query calendar → "Team sync at 2pm." Speak result to user.
3
Spine Navigate to kitchen (parallel with calendar check)
4
Spatial Coffee machine on counter, mugs in upper cabinet
5
Spine Get mug from cabinet → place under coffee machine
6
Episodic "Last time, pressed left button to brew"
7
Spine Press left button → wait for brew → pick up mug → bring to user
✓
Brain "Here's your coffee. You have a team sync at 2pm." → idle
🧳 Luggage Packing for Travel
User: "I'm traveling tomorrow. Help me pack."
1
CLI Check calendar → "Flight to Tokyo, 9am, 7-day trip, 3 meetings"
2
CLI Search web → "Tokyo weather: 18-24°C, rain on Thursday"
3
Episodic "User forgot phone charger last trip"
4
Brain Decide packing list: business attire, casual, rain jacket, toiletries, charger, adapter
5
Spatial Suitcase → bedroom closet. Shirts → wardrobe shelf 2. Rain jacket → by door.
6
Spine Navigate to bedroom → get suitcase from closet → place on bed
7
Spine Fetch items one by one: shirts, trousers, casual clothes, rain jacket, toiletries, charger, adapter
8
Brain Perceive suitcase via camera → cross-check against packing list
✓
Brain "All packed. Your flight is at 9am — want me to set an alarm?"
